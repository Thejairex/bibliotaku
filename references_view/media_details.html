Synopsis

Ten years ago, "the Gate" appeared and connected the real world with the realm of magic and monsters. To combat these vile beasts, ordinary people received superhuman powers and became known as "Hunters."

Sung Jin-Woo is an E-rank Hunter, known as the "World's Weakest." Despite his low rank, he continues to hunt to pay for his mother's medical bills. After a disastrous raid in a double dungeon, he finds himself in a hospital bed with a mysterious floating screen in front of him—a "Quest Log" that only he can see.
